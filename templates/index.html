The Alchemist's Cafe

Chicago's Trusted Merchant For Potions and Draughts

Nestled in the heart of Chicago, our cozy shop is an oasis for weary travelers and local merchants. We serve the highest quality potion brews and our specialty is our "Draughts of Soul," referred to by locals as "coffee." These potions are brewed using fresh and fragrant espresso beans, extracted in just the right way to create an opulent and tasty cup of joe. Our signature Alchemist's Flair potions, are tasty renditions of our draughts of soul but with a little added flair on behalf of our chief alchemist. We also sell ingredients so traveling alchemists can take home and brew their own potions.

So, stop in, and enjoy one of our brews (potion bottles provided).

NOT JUST "COFFEE"

Our alchemists are highly skilled professionals and crafstman, and although we specialize in Draughts of Soul, we also make other types of tinkers and potions. Besides liquid delights we also have fine eatery and delicacies for at home alchemists and travellers.
